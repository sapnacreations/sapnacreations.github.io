About Sapna Creations

My name is Tayyab. I am a professional artist who creates realistic, handmade pencil sketches on A4 paper. Each sketch is crafted with care using different pencil shades to capture details, shadows and emotion — perfect as a unique gift or keepsake.

  • Handmade — not printed
  • High-quality sketch paper (A4)
  • Simple order process via Daraz or direct contact

Photo Guidelines

  • Send a clear, close-up photo with the subject facing the camera for best detail.
  • Avoid heavy shadows, blur, or low-resolution images — clear daylight photos work best.
  • If more than one person appears in the photo, an additional charge applies per extra person.
  • After placing your order, send the chosen photo via WhatsApp with your order reference and any special requests.
